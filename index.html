How it works

Three steps. No surprises.

The same discipline I bring to enterprise systems — pointed at your business.

Step 01 — Listen

You work. I watch.

Over a call or in person, you walk me through your week — the Saturday vendor file, the emails that have to go out, the copy-paste work — or I simply sit beside you for an hour and watch how it really runs. No prep, no interrogation. I'm listening for where time leaks, not selling you a tool.

Step 02 — The plan

I do the heavy lifting

I take what I saw, do the digging on my own time, and come back with a one-page plan in plain English: quick wins you can do this week, bigger wins worth building, and what to skip. Sometimes the answer is a SaaS tool — sometimes it's a Google Sheets script or just turning on transcription you already have.

Step 03 — Execute

Build or hand off

Run the plan yourself, hand it to your team, or have me build the top items. Either way you own everything — the plan, the tools, the logins. No dependency on me.
